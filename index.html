Personal Summary

I am a researcher with an expertise in inverse problems and signal processing relevant to wave propagation and scattering. I have developed a passion to solve real-life challenges pertaining to seismic, radar and medical imaging. Currently working as a postdoctoral associate in the Imaging and Computing Group and Earth Resources Laboratory, Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT).

Current Research Interests

  • wave-equation based imaging and inversion
  • signal processing, blind deconvolution and source separation
  • seismic interferometry and its applications in both exploration and global seismology
  • parallelization and developing efficient algorithms for high performance computing in geophysical applications
